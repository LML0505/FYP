Step-by-step User Instruction

Step 1 - Select, Upload, Label

Go to Upload Count.
Click and choose single or multiple image file(s) for uploading and counting.
Label the image file(s) in Label History.
Insert the comments in the comment column.

Step 2 - Visualise presentation

After clicking on the count button, wait for count results appear in the table.
To enlarge image file, click on the image in the table.
Click on the image again to return back to normal.

Step 3 - Search, Delete

Click Upload History on the left side navigation bar.
Read all the image file(s) uploaded for count.
Click the Search button to search for the result.
Click the Delete button to delete unwanted result.

Step 4 - Label History

Click Label History button on the left navigation bar.
To see all the labeled image file(s) information.
Click the View Detail button to see all the image file(s) under the same lable.
Click the Delete button to delete unwanted result.
